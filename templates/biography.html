Michael

Mathematics Student & Pilates Instructor

My journey began in the world of numbers and logic. As a Mathematics student, I developed a deep appreciation for the elegance of data and the "why" behind complex systems—a passion that currently drives my research in R-Studio.

However, I believe in a balance between mind and body. This passion for movement led me to complete my **Mat Pilates Instructor certificate**, allowing me to share the discipline of physical alignment with others. I thrive on personal challenges, having recently completed a **half marathon** and two **10k runs**.

Beyond the classroom and the studio, I am an avid traveler and volunteer. Last year, I spent time in **Nepal**, living with a local family to assist with their daily duties, including harvesting vegetables. Immersing myself in their culture and contributing to their day-to-day lives remains one of my most transformative experiences.

Exploring the intersection of data, movement, and global community.
